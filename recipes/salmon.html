Alaska Salmon Bake with Pecan Crunch Coating

Description:

This recipe is for the Alaska Salmon Bake with Pecan Crunch Coating, a well loved excellent main course!

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Pecans
  • Bread crumbs
  • Lemon
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Mix together the pecans, bread crumbs and parsley
  2. Cover salmon with mixture
  3. Put the salmon in the oven at 400 until done
  4. Squeeze some lemon on it
  5. Take out and enjoy a delicious salmon!
