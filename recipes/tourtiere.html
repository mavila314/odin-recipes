Traditional French Canadian Tourtiere

Description:

This recipe is for the Traditional French Canadian Tourtiere, a timeless French Canadian favorite!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Pork
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Pie Crust
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Mix the ground meats, onion and spices together
  2. Place mixture in fridge until cold
  3. Put cold mixture into pie crust
  4. Bake in oven at 400 degrees until golden brown
  5. Take out and devour while hot and delicious!
