Lasagna

Description:

This recipe is for the World's Best Lasagna which is critically acclaimed and world renowned amongst the best chefs

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Pasta
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the Meat and Garlic
  3. Put the pasta and meat in a glass container
  4. Sprinkle on some cheese
  5. Put in the oven at 350 until done
  6. Take out and Eat the World's Best Lasagna
